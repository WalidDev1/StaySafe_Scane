INTRODUCTION

Welcome to StaySafe Scan ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application StaySafe Scan (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information

We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The App is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email, or contact information
  • Track your identity or create user profiles
  • Store your location data
  • Access your contacts, photos, or files
  • Collect any personally identifiable information (PII)

1.2 Sensor Data

The App accesses the following device sensors only during active scanning:

WiFi Scanner:

  • Scans available WiFi networks in your vicinity
  • Reads network names (SSIDs), signal strength, and MAC addresses
  • This data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted

Bluetooth Scanner:

  • Detects nearby Bluetooth devices
  • Reads device names, MAC addresses, and signal strength
  • This data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted

Magnetometer:

  • Measures magnetic field strength
  • Detects electromagnetic anomalies
  • This data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted

Camera:

  • Captures images to detect lens reflections
  • Images are analyzed in real-time and never saved or transmitted

Location:

  • Required by iOS and Android for WiFi scanning (system requirement)
  • We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your location
  • Location permission is used only to enable WiFi scanning functionality

IMPORTANT: All sensor data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never stored permanently
  • Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Deleted immediately after scanning stops

1.3 Technical Information

We do NOT collect any technical information such as:

  • Device model or operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports or analytics
  • IP addresses

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use your data for any purpose.

The App's sole purpose is to:

All processing happens on your device and results are shown only to you.
